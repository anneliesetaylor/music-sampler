Rex Orange County

Alexander O’Connor is a recording artist from England who is better known by his stage name Rex Orange County. O’Connor has been involved with music since his childhood, growing up learning how to play the drums and the piano. At sixteen, he started teaching himself how to play the guitar and produce his own music while attending the BRIT school, a performing arts and technology institution. He self-released his first album, Bcos U Will Never B Free, in 2015 at the age of seventeen and its success caught the attention of various musicians, resulting in the development of his professional career. In addition to Bcos U Will Never B Free, O’Connor has released two other studio albums, Apricot Princess in 2017 and Pony in 2019. He has also released numerous singles and been featured on multiple tracks in collaboration with artist such as Randy Newman, Tyler the Creator, and Benny Sings. Currently, his most popular song on Spotify is his single, “Best Friend”, which he released in 2017.

O’Connor’s music has been described as a blend of hip-hop, jazz, and pop, but primarily falls into the genres of indie pop, neo soul, alternative hip hop, and bedroom pop. The stage name of Rex Orange County stemmed from the nickname, “The OC”, that O’Connor was given by one of his teachers because of his last name.
